Weight Gain Tips

  • Consume more calories than you burn.
  • Eat nutrient-dense foods like whole grains, lean meats, and healthy fats.
  • Have 5-6 small meals a day to increase calorie intake.
  • Strength training can help you build muscle mass.
  • Drink high-calorie smoothies or shakes for extra calories.
