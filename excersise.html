Exercise Ideas to Gain Weight

Squats

Squats help build leg and glute muscles, which are key for weight gain.

Deadlifts

Deadlifts target multiple muscle groups, promoting overall mass gain.

Bench Press

Great for chest and triceps development, essential for upper body mass.

Pull-Ups

Pull-ups strengthen back and biceps, contributing to upper body bulk.

Overhead Press

Builds strong shoulders and arms, crucial for a well-rounded physique.

Lunges

Excellent for leg and glute growth, helping increase lower body strength.
